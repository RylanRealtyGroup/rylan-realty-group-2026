Seller Questions

Common questions from sellers

How do I know what my home is worth?

Market value depends on location, condition, comparable sales, and buyer demand. We can help you evaluate it.

Should I make repairs before listing?

Sometimes yes, sometimes no. We can help you decide which updates are worth doing before going on the market.

What happens after I accept an offer?

We help guide you through inspections, contingencies, timelines, paperwork, and the path to closing.
